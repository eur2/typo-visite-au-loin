Visiting From Afar – Atelier de Typographie

ENSAV La Cambre, 2020

Week of March 9, 2020: we are preparing for open house. Caption cards to be printed, our website to be finished, printed matter to be staged and the last kerning pairs to be set. These are amongst the usual rituals related to this event. In the background an epidemic is slowly starting to spread, followed by some, ignored by others. All of a sudden, bam! Social distancing, global pandemic, strict lockdown and raw inequalities suddenly become the main concerns. In the thick of it, we still have the desire to share and project our activity within the spatial empathy that typography is. So here we are, representing our department with files from our shared server alongside the projects we could document during the current lockdown. “Technology today is the campfire around which we tell our stories”, Laurie Anderson once said. Our programme carefully draws a thoughtful relationship between two practices, the editorial role of the typographer and type design. For those of you interested in the programme please let us know at atelier(at)lacambretypo.be. Through this channel, we, teachers and students, are available to answer any questions about the curriculum and the life around it.
